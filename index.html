Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Prosper.

Do I need to install anything? ▼

No. Prosper works in your browser on any device — phone, laptop, tablet. Just visit prosper-assistant.web.app.

Is my data safe? ▼

Yes. Each business gets its own isolated data. Your information is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas with industry-standard encryption.

Does Prosper work offline? ▼

Not yet — Prosper needs internet to work. We're working on an offline mode for areas with poor connectivity.

Can I use Pidgin or Nigerian English? ▼

Yes! Prosper understands Nigerian English. You can say things like "Bayo took rice on credit worth fifty thousand naira" and it'll understand.

How do I get paid through Prosper? ▼

Prosper currently helps you track who owes what. We're adding Paystack and Flutterwave payment links coming soon.

What if I need help? ▼

Reach out via WhatsApp at +234 802 058 3538 or email ogunlusiolubunmi@gmail.com. We respond within 24 hours.

Is this really free? ▼

Yes, the Free plan is genuinely free forever. We make money from businesses that grow large enough to need our paid features.

Who built this? ▼

Prosper is built by Edward Ogunlusi, a Nigerian founder based in Lagos. Made specifically for the Nigerian SME market.
